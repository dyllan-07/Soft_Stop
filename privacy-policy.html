Softstop Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Softstop is a mindfulness tool. It does not collect, transmit, or sell any of your data.

What gets stored

Softstop saves your settings — your distraction site list, snooze preferences, and display options — using Chrome's built-in storage. This syncs across your own Chrome devices via your Google account, the same way bookmarks do. Nothing is sent to us or any third party.

What we can't see

We have no server, no database, and no way to access your data. Your list of distraction sites stays on your devices.

Google Fonts

The extension popup loads a font (Cormorant Garamond) from Google Fonts. This is a standard web font request — Google may log it per their own privacy policy. No personal information from Softstop is included in this request.
